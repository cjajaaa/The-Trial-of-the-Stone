About This Story

Synopsis of the story

The story "The Trial of the Stone" is a Chinese folktale that revolves around a magical stone accused of stealing money. The villagers, who are warned to keep quiet during the trial, must learn to take the judicial system seriously. The stone's silence during the trial serves as a means to reverse the boy's misfortune and reward the thief. The story highlights the importance of evidence-based decisions and the need for fairness in judgment.
